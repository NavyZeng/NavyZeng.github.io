I am a PhD candidate at imec & Ghent University working under Prof. Wilfried Philips and Prof. Hiep Luong. My research focuses on computational imaging, tensor completion, hyperspectral imaging, and mobile intelligent photography.

Research Topic

  • Image Signal Processing: Mobile Camera, Spectral Camera
  • Computational Imaging: MSFA/Snapshot Spectral Imaging, MRI, Bayer and Non-Bayer Imaging
  • Tensor Theory: Low-Rank Tensor Models, Convex & Non-convex Optimization
